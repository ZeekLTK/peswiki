By Special::Delete Page action link/command

  1. Access Special::Delete Page from a link (e.g. from the Special::Advanced page, default menu, or user page) or by using the Special::Go to command.
  2. You'll be asked for the exact title of the page and then for confirmation
  3. When the page is deleted, you'll be returned to the page you were on before deleting the page.

By editing

  1. Edit the page you wish to delete
  2. Select everything inside the main text area and delete it
  3. You will be asked to confirm the deletion when you Save the page

References to deleted pages

Access Special::Dead Pages either directly or from Special::Advanced to remove references to the deleted page.
