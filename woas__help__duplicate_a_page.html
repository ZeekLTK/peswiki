By Special:: action link/command

  1. Access Special::Duplicate Page from a link (e.g. from the Special::Advanced page, default menu, or user page) or by using the Special::Go to command.
  2. You'll be offered the title of the current page with (duplicate) added at the end; this can be replaced with the title of your choice.
  3. Accept by pressing the OK button, or cancel by pressing the Cancel button.
  4. When the page is duplicated, you'll be on the new page you just created.
