World Cup IV

Slovenia became the first and (so far) only team to go an entire qualification campaign without conceding a goal. The team qualified from UEFA Group F with 14 points from 6 games after recording 4 wins and 2 draws. They blanked opponents Sweden, Czech Republic, and Switzerland in every match, beating the Czechs 1-0 twice and also picking up 1-0 victories over Sweden and Switzerland, as well as splitting points in 0-0 draws against the two nations as well. Siliak, Zaoric, Acimowic, and Sewasran were the goal scorers for Slovenia, each scoring the winner in their respective matches.

Slovenia continued their outstanding play in the World Cup by defeating Brazil 1-0 in their opening match. The goal came from Zaoric. In the next game they played to a 0-0 draw against Slovakia. Finally, the 8 game shutout streak ended in the final group match against Mozambique. Despite conceding twice, Slovenia still managed to win 3-2 with goals from Siliak, Acimowic, and Sewasran. Every player that had scored in qualifying had scored in the World Cup.

Slovenia again advanced as group winners and found themselves up against Sweden in the knockout round. Another 0-0 draw (the ninth shutout in ten games) proved to be the end of the road as Slovenia lost 4-5 on penalty kicks.
