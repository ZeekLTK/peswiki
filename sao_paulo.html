Introduction

Sao Paulo is a state in Brazil. It formed its own national team following Copa America I. Some of the reasons for the split included the excessive number of Brazilian players, enough to play on this team without needing to create anyone else and also to boost CONMEBOL membership, since ten teams was not ideal. Together, with Rio de Janeiro, CONMEBOL's membership increased to 12 teams.
