Introduction

This was the fourth World Cup held in WE8. The field expanded yet again, swelling to 70 nations who participated in this World Cup. Most of the new comers were from Africa, as Algeria, Angola, Congo DR, Guinea, Mozambique, and Togo attempted to qualify for the first time. Guadeloupe also entered from the CONCACAF region. Cote d'Ivoire qualified automatically as the defending champions from the previous World Cup. In the final 32 were 16 nations from Europe, 7 from Africa, 4 from South America, 3 from North America, and 2 from Asia.
  • Teams Entered: 70
  • Champion: Georgia (2nd)
  • Top Scorer: Iashvili (7)
