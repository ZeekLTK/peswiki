Introduction

Serbia is one of the two nations which made up Serbia & Montenegro, who competed in the first six World Cups. After the team failed to qualify for World Cup VI, Montenegro declared independence and the joint nation dissolved into Montenegro and Serbia. Serbia began competing with their own squad right away, and has fielded a team individually since World Cup VII, which they failed to qualify to.
