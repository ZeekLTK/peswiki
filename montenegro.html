Introduction

Montenegro is one of the two nations which made up Serbia & Montenegro, who competed in the first six World Cups. After the team failed to qualify for World Cup VI, Montenegro declared independence and the joint nation dissolved into Serbia and Montenegro. Although Serbia began competing with their own squad right away in qualification for World Cup VII, Montenegro took a bit longer to get their team up and running and did not participate individually until World Cup VIII qualifying.
