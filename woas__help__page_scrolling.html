By default, this wiki offers two choices for scrolling. You can change these settings by clicking the Advanced icon in the navigation bar and then clicking the [[Special::Options]] link.

The Fixed menu and Fixed navigation bar options can be enabled and disabled by placing a check mark in the box next to the option. Enabling them locks the navigation bar and/or the menu in place while allowing the rest of the page to scroll. Disabling them allows the entire page to scroll as one connected unit.
