Introduction

Rules

Division 1: 16 teams
  • Round 1: 4 groups of 4 - top 2 advance to Round 2 (8), 4th place to Relegation playoffs (4)
  • Round 2: 2 groups of 4 - top 2 advance to Round 3 (4)
  • Round 3: 4-team playoff for Championship
  • Relegation: 4th place teams from Round 1 playoff, 3 relegated (3)
Division 2: 20 teams
  • Round 1: 5 groups of 4 - winners advance to Round 3 (5), runners up advance to Round 2 (5), 4th place in each group Relegated (5)
  • Round 2: Best runner up auto advances to Round 3 (1), other runners up playoff for last two spots in Round 3 (2)
  • Round 3: 8-team playoff, top 3 Promote (3)
Division 3: 16 teams
  • Round 1: 4 groups of 4 - top 2 advance to Round 2 (8), 4th place in each group Relegated (4)
  • Round 2: 8-team playoff - top 5 Promote (5)
Division 4: 32 teams
  • Round 1: 8 groups of 4 - top 2 advance to Round 2 (16), 4th place to Relegation playoffs (8)
  • Round 2: 16-team playoff - top 4 Promote (4)
  • Relegation: two 4-team playoffs, winners stay up (2), losers all Relegate (6)
Division 5: 24 teams
  • Round 1: 6 groups of 4 - winners Promote (6)
