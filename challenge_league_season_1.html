Introduction

This was the first Challenge League to be contested, and was held after Confederations Cup I. A total of 107 teams participated. This was the first time Confederacy, Equatorial Guinea, Indonesia, Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, and Zambia participated in a WEFA competition.

  • Teams Entered: 107
  • Team Champion: Team 1 (Netherlands, Bolivia, Ireland, South Korea, Bulgaria, Senegal, Congo DR, Rio de Janeiro, Qatar)
  • Individual Champion: Spain (Team 12)
  • Top Scorer: Villa (18)
  • #1 Position Champion: England (Team 10)
  • #2 Position Champion: Spain (Team 12)
  • #3 Position Champion: Portugal (Team 5)
  • #4 Position Champion: South Korea (Team 1)
  • #5 Position Champion: Japan (Team 9)
  • #6 Position Champion: Senegal (Team 1)
  • #7 Position Champion: El Salvador (Team 12)
  • #8 Position Champion: Rio de Janeiro (Team 1)

Format

Teams are composed of 9 national teams, and after playing a number of challenge matches each week, those teams will play against each other in league play. For league play, national teams play their opposing position (ie. #1 vs #1, #4 vs #4, etc.) and then the team with the most individual wins is the victor.
