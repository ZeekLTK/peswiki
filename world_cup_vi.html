Introduction

This was the sixth World Cup held in WE8. The field expanded to 78 nations who participated in this World Cup. CONCACAF expanded for the fourth straight time with the additions of Canada and the West Indies, while UEFA expanded for the second consecutive time, welcoming Israel. CAF also expanded to include Burkina Faso and Cape Verde Islands. The play-offs were also brought back, with 6 qualification spots up for grab, the most since World Cup II. Like the previous competition, the defending champions Norway had to participate in qualifying. In the final 32 were 15 nations from Europe, 8 from Africa, 4 from North America, 3 from Asia, and 2 from South America.
  • Teams Entered: 78
  • Champion: Norway (2nd)
  • Top Scorer: Solskjaer (7)
