Introduction

This was the first International League. In all, 107 nations participated in the inaugural season, placed in six divisions based on performances in the first 9 World Cups as well as the first continental cups.
  • Teams Entered: 107
  • Champion:
  • Top Scorer:

Rules

Division 1: 8 teams, 2 groups of 4, group winners playoff to determine champion (1), 4th place in each group relegated (2)
Division 2: 16 teams, 4 groups of 4, play-off winners between group winners (2), 4th place in each group relegated (4)
Division 3: 16 teams, 4 groups of 4, winner of each group promotes (4), 4th place in each group relegated (4)
Division 4: 32 teams, 8 groups of 4, play-off winners between group winners (4), 4th place in each group relegated (8)
Division 5: 20 teams, 4 groups of 5, top 2 of each group promotes (8), 5th place in each group relegated (4)
Division 6: 15 teams, 3 groups of 5, winner of each group promotes (3) + winner of 3-team playoff between 2nd place (1)
