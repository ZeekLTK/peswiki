Introduction

This was the second Challenge League to be contested, and was held after Confederations Cup II. All 107 teams from the previous competition returned in addition to Benin, Haiti, Macedonia, and Sierra Leone, to boost the field to 111 total teams. Even though Guatemala and Reunion had participated in previous WEFA competitions, they were deemed "not ready" to participate in this.

Format

Teams are composed of at least 9 national teams, and after playing a number of challenge matches each week, those teams will play against each other in league play. For league play, national teams play their opposing position (ie. #1 vs #1, #4 vs #4, etc.) and then the team with the most individual wins is the victor.
