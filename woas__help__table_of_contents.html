You can insert a Table of Contents into your page by using a special wiki tag. It will be automatically populated with all the headings on a page. The placement of the Table of Contents is determined by where you put the tag in the page.

Use this code on a line by itself in a page:
[[Special::TOC]]
To add a Table of Contents to the page.

Example

If you were to create a page with these contents:
[[Special::TOC]]
= My level one heading
== My level two heading
=== My level three heading
==== My level four heading
===== My level five heading
====== My level six heading
= My next level one heading
the displayed page would have a Table of Contents at the top of the page similar to the following:

Table of Contents
My level one heading
My level two heading
My level three heading
My level four heading
My level five heading
My level six heading
My next level one heading
The exact look of the TOC is controlled by the current CSS and Javascript, both of which can be changed. The WoaS default implementation includes the ability to toggle the TOC display by clicking the Table of Contents title text.
