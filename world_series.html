Introduction

This unique tournament was held after World Cup II concluded. All nations who participated in the first two World Cups took part in the World Series along with Ghana, Mali, and Suriname who made their debut appearances in this competition. Teams were not seeded and were drawn at random in every round. The format was a 'best of seven' series where the winners advanced to the next round and the losers were eliminated. All draws were resolved by penalty shootouts.
  • Teams Entered: 62
  • Champion: Georgia (1st)
  • Top Scorer: Ashvetia (27)
